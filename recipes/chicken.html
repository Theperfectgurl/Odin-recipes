Chef John's Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Description

Chef John's Buttermilk Fried Chicken features tender chicken marinated in tangy buttermilk, coated in seasoned flour, and fried to a crispy golden perfection. The buttermilk tenderizes and adds a subtle tang, complementing the savory crust. It's a classic Southern favorite, perfect with sides like mashed potatoes or coleslaw.

Ingredients

  • For the chicken: Whole chicken (cut into eight pieces), seasonings (black pepper, salt, paprika, white pepper, dried rosemary, ground thyme, dried oregano, dried sage, and cayenne pepper), and buttermilk
  • For the seasoned flour: all-purpose flour, salt, paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, white pepper, and onion powder)
  • peanut oil for frying.

Steps

  1. Toss the chicken pieces in seasonings, then marinate in buttermilk overnight.
  2. Make the seasoned flour.
  3. Dredge each buttermilk-soaked chicken piece in flour and transfer to a plate.
  4. Fry the chicken until an instant read thermometer reads 165 degrees F.
  5. Drain on a cooling rack over a paper towel-lined baking sheet.
